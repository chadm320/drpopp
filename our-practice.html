Our Practice & Office Information

Cosmetic Dentistry Can Change Lives / The Power of Modern Dentistry

Welcome to Advanced Cosmetic & General Dentistry! We are pleased that you have chosen out practice for your oral healthcare needs and are looking forward to welcoming you in person.

Good dental health is important for maintaining your overall health. Seeking regular dental care can help prevent small issues with your teeth and gums from becoming mediacl problems. A great smile that you'll love to show off is important too. It's the first thing people notice about your face. Our team led by Dr. Popp, can give you a beautiful and healthy smile and help you keep it that way for a lifetime.

Dr. Popp’s team specializes in making your dental experiences comfortable and rewarding. We listen carefully to your needs, concerns, and desires, and establish long-term dental goals with you.

We respect your time by completing procedures in the fewest possible number of visits; in many cases, an entire dental makeover can be completed in just two appointments. We go out of our way to make these visits comfortable and relaxing by offering massage, heat therapy, and your choice of music (bring your IPod or use ours!), along with oral sedatives. General or IV sedation can be arranged for added relaxation.

These and other special touches can make a dental visit seem like a trip to the spa, with one important difference: you’ll gain permanent improvements to your health and appearance each time you come.

Our Commitment to Education

Using advanced technology and materials requires continuing education to stay knowledgeable about the latest and greatest the industry has to offer. Dr. Popp and her team dedicate time to stay on the forefront of modern dentistry and use their education and expertise to benefit their patients.

Educating patients on the importance of dental care and the best oral hygiene practices for home is also part of the Advanced Cosmetic & General Dentistry patient experience. With regular dental checkups, we can not only help preserve your teeth, but also make any treatment last as long as possible.
