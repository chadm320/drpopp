Philosophy of Care

We emphasize what is healthiest for our patients and what's going to preserve their teeth for as long as possible.

Dr. Popp and the Advanced Cosmetic & General Dentistry Team share a common philosophy of care: treat the whole person, not just his or her mouth. We value getting to know YOU. We plan to listen carefully to your needs, concerns and desires. Through this means, we can best help you achieve your dental goals.

We take the time to talk to you so we understand what you are looking for in dental care and show you all the options available when it comes to your oral health and the appearance of your teeth. We want to establish a long-term relationship with you and make you an equal partner in keeping your teeth and gums as healthy as possible.

Our comprehensive approach to care takes into account your medical and dental history, your current dental situation, and your expectations when it comes to your overall oral health. Each of our patients is unique. We strongly feel that everyone regardless of his or her age, financial means, or current dental situation.

We have built a practice to serve the needs of patients from young adulthood to senior citizens. It is never too late to begin practicing good oral hygiene. We want you to have the smile that is not only beautiful, full functional. That way you can enjoy great health and still have your steak and eat it too!
