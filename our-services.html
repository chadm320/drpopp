Our Services

At Advanced Cosmetic & General Dentistry, you will find a comprehensive array of treatment options that will be custom-designed for your individual needs. Our experienced cosmetic dentist, Dr. Popp, offers advanced procedures that can whiten your teeth, correct chips, gaps, and spaces, improve teeth misalignment in a discreet way, replace missing teeth with natural-looking results, and much more. Our dental team takes great pride in offering patients the best possible care in a warm, comfortable environment. We also provide sedation dentistry options to alleviate anxiety and help you achieve the most pleasant treatment experience possible. Dr. Popp aims to rejuvenate both the health and appearance of your teeth and gums so you can achieve a more beautiful, confident smile!

Please click on the links below for more information on our procedures, and contact Advanced Cosmetic & General Dentistry with any questions you may have, or to schedule an appointment.

Preventive Dental Care

Preventive dental care can keep your teeth and gums healthy and reduce the potential for major dental problems later in life. Our practice offers an array of general dentistry procedures that can help you maintain excellent oral health and a refreshed-looking smile. Options include professional teeth cleanings, tooth-colored fillings, periodontal care, and many other services.
