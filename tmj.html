TMJ Treatment

Headaches, facial pain, jaw clicking, and a number of other symptoms may be caused by a problem with the temporomandibular joint (TMJ). TMJ Disorder, also known as TMD, affects many individuals, and there are advanced treatment options available at Advanced Cosmetic & General Dentistry that can effectively alleviate your symptoms. Dr. Popp, can perform a comprehensive oral evaluation to determine if you have TMJ Disorder and develop a customized treatment plan designed to meet your needs.

What is TMJ Disorder?

The temporomandibular joint is located between the upper and lower jaw. If the cartilage of the joint breaks down, or if the joint is affected by teeth grinding, imbalance, arthritis, a misaligned bite, or other factors, it can ultimately cause a number of symptoms that can affect your day-to-day life and result in progressive discomfort.
